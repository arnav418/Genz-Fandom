Iron-Man

" Genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist "

Iron Man is another major superhero from Marvel Comics, created by writer Stan Lee, developed by scripter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in March 1963. Tony Stark is a billionaire industrialist, genius inventor, and philanthropist. He inherited Stark Industries, a weapons manufacturing company, from his father. During a trip to a war-torn region to demonstrate a new weapon, Stark is captured by terrorists and severely injured by shrapnel lodged near his heart. With the help of fellow prisoner Yinsen, he creates a suit of powered armor to escape captivity and sustain his heart.
